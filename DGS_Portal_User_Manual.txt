PART 1 — GETTING STARTED (FOR EVERYONE)
----------------------------------------------------------------

WHAT IS THE PORTAL?
The Divine Grace School Portal is an online platform where:
   - Teachers (Admin) can manage students, enter results,
     upload lesson notes, post announcements, and manage
     the scheme of work
   - Students can view their results, read announcements,
     access the scheme of work, and read lesson notes

HOW TO ACCESS
Open the portal by typing the school website link into any
web browser on your phone or computer. The website works on
all devices including phones, tablets, and computers.

THE LOGIN PAGE
When you open the portal you will see two login options:
   - Student  →  for students to log in
   - Admin    →  for teachers to log in

================================================================
PART 2 — ADMIN (TEACHER) GUIDE
================================================================

----------------------------------------------------------------
2.1  LOGGING IN AS ADMIN
----------------------------------------------------------------

1. On the login page click the "Admin" button at the top
2. Enter your assigned email address
   Example: basic3@divinegrace.com
3. Enter the password given to you
4. Click "Admin Sign In"

You will be taken to the Admin Dashboard.

NOTE: Each teacher has their own email login assigned to their
class. Do not share your login details with students.


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.2  THE ADMIN DASHBOARD OVERVIEW
----------------------------------------------------------------

On the left side (or bottom on mobile) you will see a menu
with the following sections:

   - Students       → manage student records
   - Results        → enter and manage student results
   - Subjects       → manage subjects per class
   - Scheme of Work → upload topics per subject and term
   - Announcements  → post notices to students
   - Lesson Notes   → write and publish lesson notes


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.3  STUDENTS TAB
----------------------------------------------------------------

VIEWING STUDENTS
When you click Students you will see a grid of all classes.
Click on any class card to see the list of students in it.
You can search for a student by name or ID using the search
box at the top of the list.

ADDING A NEW STUDENT
1. Click on the class you want to add the student to
2. Click the "Add Student" button
3. Fill in the form:
      - Full Name
      - Gender
      - Age
      - Current Class
      - Year Registered (the year they joined e.g. 2026)
4. Click "Preview ID & PIN"
   The system will automatically generate:
      - A Student ID  e.g. DGS-001-2026
      - A PIN         e.g. 5823
   Write these down — the student will use them to log in
5. Click "Confirm & Save Student" to save

EDITING A STUDENT
1. Find the student in their class list
2. Click the "Edit" button next to their name
3. Update the details and click "Save Changes"

DELETING A STUDENT
1. Find the student in their class list
2. Click the red trash (delete) button next to their name
3. Confirm the deletion
WARNING: This cannot be undone. All that student's data
will need to be re-entered if deleted by mistake.


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.4  SUBJECTS TAB
----------------------------------------------------------------

Subjects must be set up for each class before you can enter
results, scheme of work, or lesson notes for that class.

ADDING SUBJECTS TO A CLASS
1. Click the "Subjects" tab
2. Select the class from the dropdown
3. Type the subject name in the input box
4. Click "Add" or press Enter
5. Repeat for all subjects in that class
6. Subjects are saved automatically and sorted alphabetically

REMOVING A SUBJECT
Click the red trash button next to the subject name.
NOTE: Removing a subject does not delete existing results
that were already saved for that subject.


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.5  RESULTS TAB
----------------------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT — BEFORE ENTERING RESULTS:
Results Visibility toggles are at the top of the Results tab.
When a class is toggled OFF, students in that class will see
"Results Not Yet Available" instead of their scores. Toggle
classes OFF while you are uploading to avoid students seeing
incomplete results. Toggle them back ON when done.

   "Enable All"  → makes all classes visible to students
   "Disable All" → hides all results from students

ENTERING RESULTS — TWO MODES:

MODE 1: By Student (enter one student at a time)
   Best for entering full results including remarks,
   attendance, character assessment, and activities.

   Steps:
   1. Select the class
   2. Select the term (Term 1, Term 2, or Term 3)
   3. Enter the session e.g. 2025/2026
   4. A list of students in that class will appear
   5. Click "Enter Result" next to the student's name
   6. Fill in the result form (see below)
   7. Click "Save & Compute"
   The system automatically calculates positions, class
   averages, highest and lowest scores.

MODE 2: By Subject / Bulk Entry
   Best for quickly entering scores for one subject
   across all students in a class at once.

   Steps:
   1. Select the class
   2. Select the subject
   3. Select the term
   4. Enter the session
   5. A table of all students appears with CA1, CA2, Exam
      columns for each student
   6. Fill in the scores
   7. Click "Save All & Compute"

RESULT FORM — LOWER SCHOOL (Nursery to Basic 3):
   General Info:
      - Age at End of Term
      - Conduct Rating (1 to 5)
      - Days Attended
      - Days Absent
      - Term Ending Date
      - Next Term Resumption Date

   Subject Scores:
      - CA 1 (out of 20)
      - CA 2 (out of 20)
      - Exam (out of 60)
      Total is calculated automatically out of 100

   Character Assessment:
      Rate the student 1 to 5 stars for each trait:
      Behaviour, Punctuality, Staff Relationship,
      Politeness, Neatness, Obedience,
      Adjustment in School, Behaviour with Peers

   Extra-Curricular Activities:
      Enter activity names and rate performance 1 to 5 stars
      Click "+ Add Activity" to add more rows

   Remarks:
      - Teacher's Name
      - Head Teacher's Name
      - Teacher's Remark
      - Head Teacher's Remark

RESULT FORM — UPPER SCHOOL (Basic 4 to Basic 9):
   General Info:
      - Age at End of Term
      - Number of School Days
      - Days in Attendance

   Subject Scores:
      - CA 1 (out of 20)
      - CA 2 (out of 20)
      - Exam (out of 60)
      Total is calculated automatically out of 100

   Remarks:
      - Teacher's Name
      - Principal's Name
      - Teacher's Remark
      - Principal's Remark

GRADING SYSTEM:
   Lower School (Nursery – Basic 3):
      90 and above  → A  Outstanding
      80 – 89       → B  Excellent
      70 – 79       → C  Very Good
      60 – 69       → D  Good
      50 – 59       → E  Average
      40 – 49       → F  Pass
      Below 40      →    Fail

   Upper School (Basic 4 – Basic 9):
      70 and above  → A  Excellent
      60 – 69       → B  Very Good
      50 – 59       → C  Good
      40 – 49       → D  Pass
      Below 40      → F  Fail


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.6  SCHEME OF WORK TAB
----------------------------------------------------------------

The scheme of work shows students the topics they will cover
per subject per term.

UPLOADING TOPICS
1. Click the "Scheme of Work" tab
2. Select the class
3. Select the subject
4. Select the term (Term 1, Term 2, or Term 3)
5. Type each topic into the input boxes
6. Click "+ Add Topic" to add more rows
7. Click "Save Topics" when done
8. Repeat for each subject and term

NOTE: Subjects must be set up in the Subjects tab first
before they appear here.


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.7  ANNOUNCEMENTS TAB
----------------------------------------------------------------

Post notices, reminders, and news that all students will see
when they log in.

POSTING AN ANNOUNCEMENT
1. Click the "Announcements" tab
2. Enter a Title  e.g. "Mid-Term Break Notice"
3. Select a Tag (optional):
      General / Academic / Event / Holiday / Urgent
4. Type your message in the Message box
5. Click "Post"
The announcement appears immediately on the student portal.

DELETING AN ANNOUNCEMENT
Scroll down to see all posted announcements.
Click the red trash button next to the one you want to remove.


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.8  LESSON NOTES TAB
----------------------------------------------------------------

Write and publish lesson notes that students can read on
their portal.

CREATING A NEW NOTE
1. Click "Lesson Notes" in the sidebar
2. Click the "+ New Note" button
3. Select the Class, Subject, and Term
4. Type your note in the editor
   You can format text using the toolbar:
      Bold, Italic, Underline, Headings, Lists, Tables
   You can also paste or drag and drop images directly
   into the editor
5. Click "Save Note"

EDITING AN EXISTING NOTE
1. Find the note in the list on the left
2. Click on it to open it
3. Click the "Edit" button
4. Make your changes and click "Save Note"

DELETING A NOTE
1. Open the note
2. Click "Delete"
3. Confirm the deletion

NOTE: One note is stored per class/subject/term combination.
If you save a note for the same class, subject, and term
again it will update the existing note.

LOGGING OUT
Click "Sign Out" at the bottom of the sidebar to log out
safely when you are done.


================================================================
PART 3 — STUDENT GUIDE
================================================================

----------------------------------------------------------------
3.1  LOGGING IN AS A STUDENT
----------------------------------------------------------------

1. Open the portal website link
2. Make sure "Student" is selected at the top of the
   login page (it is selected by default)
3. Enter your Student ID  e.g. DGS-001-2026
4. Enter your 4-digit PIN
5. Click "Sign In"

Your Student ID and PIN were given to you by your school.
If you have lost them contact your class teacher.


----------------------------------------------------------------
3.2  THE STUDENT DASHBOARD
----------------------------------------------------------------

After logging in you will see your Dashboard which shows:
   - Your name and class at the top
   - Your profile information
   - A summary of your latest results
   - Recent school announcements

At the top (or bottom on mobile) you will see the menu:
   - Dashboard      → your home page
   - My Results     → view your full report card
   - Scheme of Work → see topics covered in your class
   - Announcements  → read school news and notices


----------------------------------------------------------------
3.3  MY RESULTS PAGE
----------------------------------------------------------------

1. Click "My Results" from the menu
2. Select the Session  e.g. 2025/2026
3. Select the Class
4. Select the Term (Term 1, Term 2, or Term 3)
Your result will load showing:
   - Your average score and position in class
   - Your scores for each subject
   - Character assessment (lower school)
   - Extra-curricular activities (lower school)
   - Teacher and Head Teacher remarks

PRINTING YOUR RESULT
Click the "Print Result" button at the bottom of the page
to print or save your result as a PDF.

NOTE: If you see "Results Not Yet Available" it means your
teacher has not released the results yet. Check back later.


----------------------------------------------------------------
3.4  SCHEME OF WORK PAGE
----------------------------------------------------------------

1. Click "Scheme of Work" from the menu
2. Your class scheme loads automatically
3. Click on any subject to expand and see the topics
   covered across Term 1, Term 2, and Term 3


----------------------------------------------------------------
3.5  ANNOUNCEMENTS PAGE
----------------------------------------------------------------

Click "Announcements" from the menu to read all school
notices, event updates, and important messages from the
school. The most recent announcements appear at the top.


----------------------------------------------------------------
3.6  LOGGING OUT
----------------------------------------------------------------

Click the "Logout" button at the bottom of the sidebar
(or in the menu on mobile) to log out of your account.
Always log out when using a shared or public device.


================================================================
PART 4 — TIPS AND REMINDERS
================================================================

FOR TEACHERS:
   - Always set up Subjects for a class before entering
     results or uploading scheme of work
   - Toggle results visibility OFF before uploading to avoid
     students seeing incomplete data
   - Each teacher should use only their own login email
   - If you make a mistake on a result you can re-open it
     and save again — it will overwrite the previous entry
   - The system automatically calculates positions and
     averages whenever you save a result

FOR STUDENTS:
   - Your Student ID and PIN are unique to you — do not
     share them with others
   - Results only appear after your teacher has uploaded
     and released them
   - You can print your result directly from the portal
     by clicking "Print Result"
   - The portal works on any phone or computer with
     internet access

FOR THE SCHOOL:
   - Student IDs follow the format:  DGS-001-2026
     (School code - serial number - year registered)
   - PINs are automatically generated and linked to
     each student's ID
   - Keep a record of each student's ID and PIN in case
     they forget
